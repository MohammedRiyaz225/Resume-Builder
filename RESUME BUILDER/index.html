Frequently Asked Questions

What is Resu-Master’s mission?

How can I make my resume stand out?

You can make your resume stand out by matching your skills to the job description. Employers will be impressed if you highlight your most relevant skills and experience.

What is the best resume template?

The best resume template is the one that best serves your needs and purpose by offering a layout for your resume sections that’s easy for recruiters to follow. A good resume template should always include the five essential resume sections: contact information, resume summary or resume objective, work experience, skills and education.

How long should a resume be?

As a general rule, most recruiters recommend resumes that are one-page long. A one-page resume is much easier and quicker to read and share with colleagues. However, in recent years, recruiters have become more flexible with page length in favor of resumes that genuinely capture a candidate’s qualifications.

What is a resume builder?

A resume builder is an online tool that generates a resume in less time than it takes to write it yourself. Resu-Master’s Resume Builder utilizes your desired job title and years of experience to suggest personalized content for all your resume sections, so your effort is minimal.

How do I determine to Select Resume Skills?

The best way to determine which skills to use on your resume is to read the job description carefully and take note of which requirements match your skillset. List those skills on your resume, taking care to use a mix of soft and hard skills to convey that you are well-rounded.

Should I customize my resume for each job?

Absolutely. You should customize your resume for each job to have the best chance of getting a callback for an interview. A one-size-fits-all resume is a myth, and will likely yield different results than you want. By customizing your resume for each job, you ensure that you highlight the skills and experiences the employer needs for that particular role.
